Is it because people could no longer afford the gas prices (which I doubt) or they need a change in technology of their ride? The Model S is too expensive compared to gas expenses per month. I don’t think it is worth it unless all hydrocarbons are depleted from earth. What does others think?

People don’t buy cars based on economics. If so, why would anyone pay $100K+ for this (Maserati)?

When they can have this for $20K (Kia)?

Same number of doors, same number of seats, similar styling, better gas mileage, lower maintenace costs.

The purchase of cars is typically not a rational, economic decision. Tesla has done a great job of creating a desirable car - it is as desirable to many, many people as other non-electric cars in the same price range - it’s sexy, and has great performance.

Oh, and it’s also electric. Which many people desire not only to save money on gas, but out of a belief that it is a better choice for the environment.
